Start setup
Step 1

Create an account

If you are new to Discord, open the app or website, choose Register, and create an account with an email address you can access right away.

Step 2

Log in

If you already have an account, sign in through the Discord app or website and confirm you can access your home screen before opening the invite.

Use the same account you want connected to the server long term.

Reset your password first if login is failing.

Turn on two-factor authentication later if your community requires it.

Troubleshooting

Can’t create an account: check for email verification messages or try another browser.

Can’t log in: use password reset and confirm you are on the correct account.

Can’t see channels: complete verification or contact a moderator.

Notifications are overwhelming: update server notification settings after joining.
